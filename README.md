Why Hallmarks Matter

Hallmarks are the tiny stamps you often see on gold, silver, and platinum jewellery. These small marks can tell you a surprising amount about a piece — including the metal quality, where it was made, and sometimes even the exact year it was produced.

For antique jewellery especially, hallmarks are incredibly important. They help identify the history and authenticity of a piece and can make a big difference to its value. A small mark inside a ring or on the back of a brooch can reveal a whole story.

Even with modern jewellery, hallmarks confirm that the metal is genuine, such as sterling silver or 18-carat gold.

Why I Created This App

I created this app because hallmark identification can be confusing. The marks are tiny, there are thousands of them, and most people don’t know where to start.

This app is designed to make that process much easier.

About the App

The app is a free tool to help people identify hallmarks on jewellery. It’s designed for collectors, antique dealers, jewellers, and anyone who simply wants to know more about a piece they own.

My aim is to make hallmark knowledge accessible to everyone, so that anyone can start uncovering the history behind their jewellery.
